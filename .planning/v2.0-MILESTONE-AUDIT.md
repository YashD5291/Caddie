## Verdict

`gaps_found` — one critical (CAL-02 prompt path), one feature-absent (CAL-03, needs product decision: the implemented design prompts at start; the original requirement wanted 2-min-before). Remaining deviations are acceptable tech debt.

**Fix direction for CAL-02:** special-case calendar signals in `MeetingDetector.handleSignal` — when a `.googleCalendar` signal with a `calendarEventID` arrives and no meeting is current, fire `onMeetingPrompt` directly instead of routing through the two-signal `DecisionEngine` (whose fusion rule presumes monitors that are intentionally disabled). Update `testGoogleCalendarAloneDoesNotTrigger` to assert the corrected behavior.
